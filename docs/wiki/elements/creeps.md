# Creeps (Neutral Droids)

Creeps are neutral droids (no team) that inhabit the "jungle" or off-lane areas of maps. Unlike lane droids, they do not push objectives and only attack when provoked or when a player enters their territory.

## Role & Mechanics

- **Health Restoration:** The primary purpose of creeps is to provide sustain. When killed, creeps drop **health pickups**, allowing heroes to heal without returning to base.
- **Flux Drops:** Like all entities, when creeps die to a non-hero cause (e.g. traps), their Flux drops on the ground. When killed by a hero, the Flux flies directly to the killer.
- **Respawn:** Creeps spawn from a `creepDen` entity and respawn on a timer after being killed.

## Boss Creep

The boss creep is a large neutral objective on specific maps. 
- **Reward:** Killing it heals the killer for a massive 3000 HP and pays 20 Flux (currency) to the *entire killing team*.

## Scripted Hazards

- **Sandworm:** A zone-anchored scripted hazard (such as on Sorona) that telegraphs its attack and devours players, acting as an environmental trap rather than a standard creep.
