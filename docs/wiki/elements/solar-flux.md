# Solar & Flux (Currency)

In the Cosmonauts universe, **Flux** is the single in-game currency, replacing "Solar" from the original Awesomenauts game. 

## Obtaining Flux

Flux is represented as physical cubes in the game world. It is used exclusively to purchase ability upgrades and stat modifiers at the team's Shop.

- **Silver Flux Cube:** Worth 1 Flux.
- **Golden Flux Cube:** Worth 5 Flux.

### Drop Rules
1. **Hero Kills:** When an Awesomenaut kills an enemy hero, droid, or creep, the resulting Flux automatically flies directly to the killer.
2. **Environment/Turret Kills:** When a unit dies to a non-hero cause (e.g., killed by a Turret, a Trap, or a Kill Zone), the Flux simply drops on the ground as physical pickups, waiting for any player to walk over and collect them.
3. **Ambient Pickups:** Map designers can place Flux cubes directly onto the map with a `respawnTime`. This creates ambient income routes, typically found in the jungle or off-lane areas, rewarding map control and roaming.
4. **Boss Creep Bounty:** Defeating the Boss Creep instantly pays a bounty of 20 Flux to the *entire* killing team.

## Shared Economy vs Individual

Flux is generally an individual resource used for personal upgrades. Controlling ambient flux and securing last-hits on droids is key to out-scaling the enemy team.
