# AI Station 205

AI Station 205 is a highly structured 2-lane map with a dynamic hazard that heavily splits the map.

## Layout & Geography

- **The Inferno Device:** The map is uniquely separated by a central Inferno Device, making lane transitions difficult. 
  - The device activates with a 2-second warning and fires for 8 seconds (dealing 400 DPS) before resting for 21 seconds.
  - A healthpack resides dangerously inside the flame zone.
- **Hide Areas:** There are **zero** hide areas on this map, completely removing stealth ambushes from standard gameplay.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Neutral Creeps:** The top lane contests a jungle area containing 3 "Service-Bot" creeps.
- **Droid Releasers:** Each team has a Droid Releaser button placed directly next to their top turret. Activating this button spawns up to 3 flying Hummingbird Droids (which uniquely attack the turret). The releaser restocks every 15 seconds.
