# AI Station 205

AI Station 205 is an enclosed, highly structured space station map defined by a massive central hazard and two immense vertical pillars. It forces heavy convergence in a dangerous porous center.

## Layout & Geography

- **Thick Ceilings & Floors:** The map is completely enclosed with solid terrain at the top and bottom.
- **The Central Pillars:** Two massive vertical solid terrain pillars divide the map into the Red Zone, Central Hazard Shaft, and Blue Zone.
- **The Pits:** Between the inner bases and the central pillars on the bottom lane are sunken pits crossed by glass platforms.
- **Hide Areas:** Hide Zones are located within the sunken lane pits, providing mid-lane ambush opportunities for defenders.
- **Glass Platforms:** Heavy use of glass platforms bridge the lane pits and create a stepped, vertical combat bridge across the central shaft.
- **Jumpers:** Vertical recovery jumpers are placed at the bottom of the sunken pits, alongside standard base elevators.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Asymmetrical Defenses:** 
  - **Bottom Path:** Attackers must push through an Inner Turret (near base) and an Outer Turret (mounted on the lower central pillar).
  - **Top Path:** Only features Outer Turrets mounted on the upper central pillars. There are no inner top turrets.
- **The Inferno Device:** The massive central gap is a timed death trap. Thick pillars house flamethrowers that periodically shoot beams of fire (dealing 400 DPS) across the entire gap, turning the central glass platforms into a hazard. Activates with a 2-second warning, fires for 8 seconds, rests for 21 seconds.
- **High-Risk Pickups:** A high-value Health Pickup is suspended precisely in the dead center of the Inferno Device.
- **Top Lane & Droid Releasers:** The top lane is short. Instead of base spawns, activating Droid Releasers (restocking every 15s) spawns Humming Droids behind the top turrets, which then drop directly into the central gap.
- **Neutral Creeps:** The very top of the central shaft features high glass platforms where neutral "Service-Bot" creeps roam, dropping small health/solar pickups.
