# AI Station 404

AI Station 404 was the second map of the game. It is one of the three original maps. It was later replaced by AI Station 205 in ranked games, becoming available only in custom and practice matches.

## Background
This AI station is built on the surface of an asteroid of solid Solar.

These kind of asteroids are quite rare and it's no surprise the robots quickly tried to conquer it after discovery. The space station is now one of the most high tech weapon facilities in the galaxy. Flagship of the facility is the chromium gravitor device which manipulates gravity for experimental research.

## Map Features

### Anti Gravity Field
There is a large anti-gravity field in the middle of AI Station 404. It significantly alters the jumping height and falling speed of Awesomenauts, allowing for extended aerial maneuvers and floating combat.

### Droid Releaser
The map features specific mechanics to control or throttle the release of friendly droids, affecting the pushing dynamics of the lanes.

### Creep Area
A specialized jungle section exists where neutral creeps spawn, providing an alternate source of health and Solar.
