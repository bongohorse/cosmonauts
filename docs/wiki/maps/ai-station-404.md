# AI Station 404

AI Station 404 is a very short 1-lane map primarily relegated to Custom or Practice games rather than standard competitive rotation.

## Layout & Geography

- **Single Lane:** Features only one primary lane of combat.
- **Anti-Gravity Field:** The defining feature is a massive central Anti-Gravity field. This field drastically affects both player jump heights/air control and the physical arc of projectiles.
- **Hide Areas:** There are **zero** hide areas on this map.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Neutral Creeps:** The upper jungle area contains 4 Neutral "Service-Bot" Creeps.
- **Droid Releasers:** Each team has a Droid Releaser button next to their top turret that spawns Hummingbird Droids (up to 3 max, restocks every 10 seconds). Uniquely, these Hummingbirds are programmed to exclusively path towards and attack the inner bottom turret.
