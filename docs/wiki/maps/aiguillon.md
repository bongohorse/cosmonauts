# Aiguillon

Aiguillon is a cramped, stealth-focused map that emphasizes map control, ganking, and careful navigation.

## Layout & Geography

- **Cramped Lanes:** The map features very tight pathways, extensive use of glass platforms, moving platforms, and jump pads, making traversal uniquely tricky.
- **Hide Areas:** Aiguillon features an extreme abundance of Hide Areas (6 in total):
  - One large hide area at the top (contains a healthpack).
  - One hide area at the bottom (contains a healthpack).
  - One hide area in front of *each* outer bottom turret.
  - One hide area behind *each* team's energy wall.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Neutral Creeps:** The map contains 6 "Solar Crab" creeps (one behind each team wall, two in front of each team wall).
- **The Stealth Orb:** The defining feature of Aiguillon is the central Stealth Orb. Picking it up grants 20 seconds of complete invisibility. It respawns every 60 seconds.
