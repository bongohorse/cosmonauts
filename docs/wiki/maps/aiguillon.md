# Aiguillon

Aiguillon is the fourth map of the game, released on April 9th, 2013 with Patch 1.17. 

## Background
Legend tells the story of a giant cranky porcupine that wandered through space. Furious at the sun for burning his eyes, he charged and killed both the light and himself. 

His prickly remains now orbit the dead sun and gave home to the Kremzon, a crude and devious species that spend most of their time in bustling cabarets and smokey cafés. Aiguillon's thorny rings conceal a mythical temple, rumored to hold the power of invisibility.

## Map Features

### Stealth Orb
Aiguillon has a special pickup called the **Stealth Orb**. It grants the player 20 seconds of invisibility. Attacking or taking damage typically reveals the player.

### Hide Areas
The map features specific zones covered by foreground foliage, allowing players to hide from enemies passing by.

### Creep Areas
Neutral creeps spawn in designated jungle zones on the map, providing opportunities to farm Solar or regenerate health via lifesteal.
