# Holiday Themes

Throughout the year, the Awesomenauts maps occasionally feature festive holiday overlays.

## Awesomenauts Anniversary
Adds pennons and presents on multiple map locations and causes colored paper to fall down on certain areas of the maps.
- **Affected Maps:** Ribbit IV, Sorona, AI Station 404, Aiguillon.

## Valentine's Day
Adds flowers, hearts, and presents on multiple map locations and causes hearts to fall down on certain areas of the maps.
- **Affected Maps:** Ribbit IV, Sorona, AI Station 404.
