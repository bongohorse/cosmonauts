# Ribbit IV

Ribbit IV is a classic 2-lane swamp map and serves as one of the fundamental competitive environments in Cosmonauts.

## Layout & Geography

- **Lanes:** Features a standard top and bottom lane, though the top lane is noticeably shorter than the bottom lane.
- **Verticality:** Aerial space is relatively limited. The middle of the map features a massive bottom-to-top jump pad for rapid vertical traversal.
- **Hide Areas:** There are exactly 3 Hide Areas on the map:
  - Two interconnected hide areas at the bottom of the map.
  - One large hide area at the top of the map.
  - *Map-Specific Tell:* Entering a hide area on Ribbit IV causes visible leaves to fall, giving observant opponents a clue.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Neutral Creeps:** The central lush jungle contains 4 Small Beast Creeps.
- **Boss Creeps:** Ribbit IV uniquely features **two** Boss Creeps (acid-spitting monsters), positioned symmetrically on each side of the map. Defeating one grants a massive 5000 HP heal, 60 Flux (distributed to the team), and 50 global XP.
