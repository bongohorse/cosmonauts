# Sorona

Sorona is a large 2-lane wasteland map with a massive central open area that heavily favors flying characters.

## Layout & Geography

- **Central Area:** The middle of the map features a wide-open area with bumpers, platforms, and a central jump pad (boosters).
- **Hide Areas:** A massive central Hide Area sits above the bottom lane's button. It grants strategic access to both lanes and contains a healthpack.
  - *Map-Specific Tell:* Entering a hide area on Sorona causes very subtle dust to fall.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Neutral Creeps:** Contains 4 worm creeps (positioned symmetrically, two on the left and two on the right, above the turrets).
- **The Sandworm:** The middle of the bottom lane features a button that opens a shaft to a Giant Sandworm. 
  - The worm stays exposed as long as the button is held down.
  - It instantly kills any Awesomenaut or Droid that touches it (forcing them to drop all their carried Flux). This is a primary tool for environmental kills and wave clearing.
