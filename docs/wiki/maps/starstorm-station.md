# Starstorm Station

Starstorm Station is a sprawling, derelict battle station that heavily favors characters with strong vertical mobility or free flight.

## Layout & Geography

- **Vertical Map:** The map is extremely large vertically, featuring high ceilings and multiple tiers of engagement.
- **Teleporters:** Features 6 teleporters (3 entry portals, 3 exit portals). Crucially, these teleporters conserve player momentum upon exiting, allowing for strategic slingshot maneuvers.
- **The Pit:** The bottom lane features a massive waste disposal shaft with a bottomless pit that acts as an instakill zone.
- **Hide Areas:** Contains only 1 Hide Area, located directly above the bottomless pit.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **The Golden Cube:** The bottom area contains a unique golden Flux cube worth 5 Flux.
- **Neutral Creeps:** The station is patrolled by 4 Starstorm Robot Creeps (Sscreeps).
- **Turret Vulnerability:** Starstorm Station is unique in its structural defense: the inner top turrets are completely exposed from the start of the match. Destroying just one of them completely disables the Drill's protective energy wall, effectively bypassing the normal lane pushing requirements.
