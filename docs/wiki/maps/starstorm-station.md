# Starstorm Station

Starstorm Station is a massive, horizontally elongated spaceship hovering in space, heavily favoring characters with strong vertical mobility. The ship is divided into two distinct, parallel decks with a highly concentrated double-layered defense system.

## Layout & Geography

- **Two Decks:** The Grid/Solid Terrain forms the thick outer hull and divides the main play area into an upper and lower deck.
- **Central Shaft:** A massive, thick horizontal block of solid terrain runs through the middle of the ship, completely separating the top and bottom lanes except for a critical, open vertical shaft in the dead center.
- **The Lower Island & Void:** A small, detached island of solid terrain is suspended far below the main hull. The bottomless void acts as an instakill zone for anyone who falls and misses the recovery jumpers.
- **Teleporters:** Features 6 teleporters (3 entry portals, 3 exit portals). Crucially, these teleporters conserve player momentum upon exiting, allowing for strategic slingshot maneuvers.
- **Hide Areas:** 
  - **The Central Core:** A massive primary Hide Zone occupies the exact center of the Bottom Lane, directly between opposing front turrets.
  - **Turret Flanks:** Smaller Hide Zones on raised ledges behind the front bottom turrets.
  - **Top Deck Edges:** Hide Zones present near solid terrain drop-offs on the top lane.

## Map Elements & Objectives

- **Double-Layered Defense:** Outer and inner turrets are positioned in both lanes, requiring attackers to destroy two turrets in a single lane to breach the base. Inner top turrets are uniquely exposed, allowing a bypass of normal lane pushing requirements if destroyed early.
- **The Golden Cube / Central Orb:** The absolute center bottom area contains a distinct glowing orb mechanism and a unique Golden Flux cube worth 5 Flux.
- **Neutral Creeps:** Patrolled by 4 Starstorm Robot Creeps (Sscreeps).
- **Glass Platforms:** Bridging the central top lane gap, floating high above the top deck, and forming a stepped path down to the lower island.
- **Jumpers:** Essential base elevators (vertical launch tubes) and angled jumpers on the lower island for recovery.
