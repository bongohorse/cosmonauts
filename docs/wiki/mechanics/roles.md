# Character Roles

Awesomenauts are categorized into five primary roles. While roles are not strictly binding, they dictate base stat scaling profiles and general playstyles.

## Assassin
- **Traits:** Possess lower than average health but high burst damage and mobility. Adept at chasing, escaping, and eliminating isolated or vulnerable targets.
- **Examples:** Froggy G, Leon Chameleon, Penny Fox, Vinnie & Spike.

## Fighter
- **Traits:** Average health pools with strong dueling capabilities. Typically most effective in melee or short-range brawls.
- **Examples:** Admiral Swiggins, Ayla, Chucho Krokk, Dizzy, Jimmy & the LUX5000, Rocco, Sheriff Lonestar, Skølldir, Smiles, Ted McPain.

## Harasser
- **Traits:** Rely on long-range attacks and relatively short-cooldown abilities. They wear down enemy health from a safe distance to force retreats or set up kills for assassins. Their kits usually include tools to keep enemies away.
- **Examples:** Coco Nebulon, Nibbs, Gnaw, Max Focus, Raelynn, Skree, Sentry X-58.

## Support
- **Traits:** Highly valuable in teamfights but often vulnerable alone. They bring teamfight-changing utility tools (heals, massive shields, unique crowd control) and typically scale very well into the late game.
- **Examples:** Deadlift, Genji, Ix the Interloper, Professor Yoolip, Voltar the Omniscient, Yuri.

## Tank
- **Traits:** Massive health pools. Their kits are designed to mitigate, absorb, or nullify incoming damage. They are dangerous to engage directly and excel at peeling for vulnerable teammates.
- **Examples:** Clunk, Derpl Zork, Scoop of Justice, Sentry X-58.
