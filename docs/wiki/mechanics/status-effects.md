# Status Effects & Modifiers

During combat, characters can apply various status effects to themselves, allies, or enemies. These alter stats, restrict movement, or provide sustain.

## Buffs (Positive Effects)

- **Haste:** Temporarily increases base movement speed.
- **Shield:** Provides damage reduction or a temporary barrier that absorbs incoming damage.
- **Heal-over-time (HoT):** Gradually restores health over a set duration.

## Debuffs (Negative Effects)

- **Snare / Root:** Completely prevents the target from moving horizontally or vertically, though they can usually still attack.
- **Slow:** Reduces the target's movement speed by a percentage.
- **Stun:** A hard crowd-control effect that prevents *both* movement and attacking/casting abilities.
- **Blind:** A visual debuff that obscures the affected player's screen or hides the UI/minimap.
- **Silence:** Prevents the target from casting active abilities, though they can still move and use auto-attacks.

## Advanced Modifiers

- **Lifesteal:** Returns a percentage of the damage dealt directly to the attacker as health. This is vital for the sustain of certain brawlers and assassins.
- **Time Modifier:** Unique abilities (like Yuri's Time Warp) that alter the flow of time locally. This can slow down enemy movement and projectiles within a specific area, or speed up allies.
